Beef Mince

Description

Quick and easy beef mince

Can be used for tacos, bowls, toasted wraps, and more

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Tomato Paste
  • Seasoning
  • Corn
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Add beef to a pan on high heat and break it up into mince
  2. Cook until browned
  3. Add tomato paste, seasoning, Corn, and Water
  4. Mix add-ins evenly
  5. Cook until all combined and fragrant
