Lasagna


Description

A pasta dish with layers of pasta sheets, beef, cheese, and bechamel sauce

Quick, easy, healthy, and very tasty

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Tomato
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Cook beef and garlic over medium heat
  3. Stir in tomato and black pepper
  4. Boil lasagna sheets in water for 8 to 10 minutes
  5. Preheat oven to 190° C
  6. Layer lasagna sheets, then beef and bechamel sauce, repeat once
  7. Bake in oven for 25 minutes
