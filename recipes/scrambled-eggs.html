Scrambled Eggs

Description

Quick and easy to cook

Delicious and fluffy

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk
  2. When eggs are completely whisked together, add milk, salt, and pepper and whisk again
  3. When whisking is done, add butter to a low heat pan followed by egg mixture
  4. Use a spatula to move mixture around pan to avoid burning
  5. When cooking is nearly done, turn off stove and let the heat from the pan finish cooking
  6. Move eggs from pan to plate and enjoy
